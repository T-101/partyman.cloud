About

The short history.

  • Summer 2020

    Our Humble Beginnings

    The first version was developed. It and all it's bugs were welcomed to the summer edition of Bukka. Livevote was a mess, but worked out in the end!

  • Autumn 2020

    The first trial

    It was decided to try out Partyman at the Online edition of Assembly Summer 2020, to replace the dated old system. Bugs were squashed in a casual "live coding, test in production" -fashion.

  • 2021

    Transition to Full Service

    Partyman was used at the Assembly Winter 2021 event and more parties became interested. A slide player to was added to ease the well-known problem at smaller parties. Bug squashing never stop!

  • 2022

    Phase Two Expansion

    Assembly Summer 2022 going full strength, and we are ready!
